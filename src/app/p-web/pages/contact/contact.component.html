How to Reach Us

Our office is conveniently located in the Industrial Zone of Ho Chi Minh City, easily accessible by public transportation and private vehicles.

By Car:

From the city center, take Highway 1A towards the Industrial Zone. Our office is located in Building A3.

By Public Transport:

Take Bus #54 or #56 from the city center to the Industrial Zone Bus Stop. Our office is a 5-minute walk from the bus stop.
